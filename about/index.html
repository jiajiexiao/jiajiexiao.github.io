About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I’m Jiajie “JJ” Xiao, and I’m excited to share a bit about myself, my research interests, and my professional experiences.

Profile Summary#

I am a researcher with a passion for improving healthcare with data-driven innovations. Currently, I serve as a Staff Machine Learning Scientist at Freenome, building multi-domain (e.g. multiomics, EHR) models to enable sensitive and specific blood-based early cancer diagnosis. My experience and interests also extend to therapeutic development such as ML-guided protein design and drug discovery, gained during my time at GSK and WFU.

I adapt state-of-the-art AI/ML advancements to overcome challenges in a variety of high-dimensional noisy data and drive data-informed decision-making. I am a fast learner, active researcher and collaborative servant leader who enjoys tackling complex, real-world problems with cross-function teams. I am committed to using my expertise to make a positive impact on people’s lives.

Research Interests#

As a physicist by training, I am particularly interested in machine learning in recent years. My current work focuses on adapting advanced machine learning approaches to address urgent medical needs. I find joy in the process of research on cutting-edge AI/ML and their applications that facilitate life-saving innovations. I am motivated by the potential impact of my research on AI4Science.

My AI/ML research areas include:

  • Domain Generalization and Robust Representation Learning
  • Generative Modeling and Reinforcement Learning
  • Instability and Safety
